Searching

In order to search for textbooks type keywords into the search box on the home page. You can search for your textbooks by typing your class name, number, textbook name, textbook isbn, or textbook author. Searching by isbn is the most efficient way of looking for a textbook. After searching you will be presented with a list of textbooks. Click on the arrow on the right side of one of the a box to go to the textbook details page.
